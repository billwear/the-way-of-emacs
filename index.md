# The Way of Emacs

Emacs is not just an editor. It's a way of working, and for some of us,
a way of thinking. I call that the way of Emacs.

This site is not here to convert you. If you already know Emacs,
you'll find useful things here: configurations, workflows, and honest
explanations of why things work the way they do.

If you're Emacs-curious but intimidated, you're in the right place.
My goal is to show you that this isn't magic, just plain thinking all the
way down.

No fluff. Certainly no religion. Just the work.
