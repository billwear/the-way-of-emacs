about the way of emacs

why this exists

Emacs is more than a text editor—it’s a way of thinking. It teaches modularity, adaptability, and signal clarity in a noisy world. But you don’t have to learn Emacs to benefit from its philosophy.

The Way of Emacs is about applying these principles to life. This is a space for deep thinkers, builders, and those who want control over their own systems — both digital and personal.

who i am

I’m Bill. I’ve spent decades working with Unix, Emacs, and technology. But my real passion is figuring out how to use tools — not just for efficiency, but for better thinking. I believe that the right mental models, like the ones Emacs embodies, can make a real difference in how we work and live.

what you’ll find here

  • Essays on modularity, signal-vs-noise thinking, and personal mastery.
  • Lessons from Emacs, Unix, and open-source philosophy that apply beyond tech.
  • Strategies for designing your own life systems, rather than inheriting someone else’s defaults.
  • A growing library of practical tools and insights to help you build a more intentional way of living.

join the journey

This isn’t a site about Emacs, and it’s not a self-help blog. It’s something in between — a place to explore how the right tools and mindset can change everything.

Read, apply, experiment. There’s no one true way — but there are better ways. Let’s find them.
