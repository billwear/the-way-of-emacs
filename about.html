about the way of emacs

There are things I want to say. This site is a place for me to say them. I have no idea who needs or wants to hear them, but it seems like someone else might get something out of them, so I'm sharing.

At best, I'm an amateur philosopher. Now in my mid-sixties, and feeling like I'm facing extinction in the job market, I'm trying to switch to a mode of interacting that I hope will keep me going.

I've been thinking about these things for over half a century. Liberal Christian philosophy caught my attention when I was nine, and while the tenets didn't stick, the idea of a workable and coherent credo did.

What's emerged doesn't seem to bear the stamp of any particular school of thought. These are just things I want to talk about.
