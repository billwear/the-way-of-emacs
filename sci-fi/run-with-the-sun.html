🧠 Building a Pivot Point

April 6, 2025 — Drift Season, Day of Planning

🧠 Core Insight

“All the others run with the sun.”

This line came to me while crawling through attic insulation and trying not to scream at my house wiring. One rogue wire ran North-South, while everything else in the house ran East-West — sun-aligned. I muttered the line, and then everything in my novel snapped into place.

Suddenly, Neptune’s magnetic field aligned with my circuit box. That’s how this works.

🛠️ Real-World Spark

In the attic of our legacy home, I was tracing a rogue circuit to the dining room. After hours of crawling and testing, I found the outlier — a wire running south, hidden and long.

“All the others run with the sun.”

The pattern stuck. The only N-S line. Everything else? E-W. Like photosynthesis. Like gravity. Like storylines.

📓 Emologent Fragment

"Emologent" stands for "Emotional Log Entry," which is something I do every morning when I wake up. Build the day around my mood, so that they work together. You can imagine the other permutations of this, mostly all bad.

  • Mood: 4
  • Energy: Medium
  • Social Battery: Empty
  • Clarity: Fuzzy
  • Emotions: Stirring, Masking, Housework Apathy
  • Physical: Muscle aches, sleep saturation
  • Mantra: “Don’t shine. Just align.”

This “meh” mood wasn’t a block—it was a mirror. It became Lira’s mood. Her weather system. The key to the artifact.
