The Way of Emacs

I live inside Emacs — for writing, thinking, planning, and remembering. If you’ve ever wondered whether one tool could help you organize your entire creative life, you’re in the right place.

I’m a technical writer by trade, a sci-fi dreamer by night, and someone who got tired of making dumb mistakes just because my brain runs faster than my process. So I built a system inside Emacs that slows me down just enough to get good work done.

What you'll find here:

  • Writing workflows: How I outline, draft, revise, and ship longform work in org-mode
  • Real-world tools: Capture templates, agenda views, custom commands — built for friction, not fantasy
  • A philosophy: Small changes. Gentle attention. The art of sustainable focus.

What I’m building:

  • The Emacs Writer’s Toolkit: A pay-what-you-want starter kit (coming soon)
  • Workshops: Live walkthroughs and Q&A (starting this summer)
  • Consulting: For writers and teams who want to build workflows that actually work
